Our Journey

Spreading Smiles India is dedicated to bringing positive change through sustainable initiatives focused on education, healthcare, and sanitation.

"Every act of kindness is a stepping stone to a better world."

Our Mission

Founded on September 23, 2017, Spreading Smiles India began with a simple belief: that everyone deserves dignity, opportunity, and hope. What started as a small group of friends has blossomed into a family of over 100 dedicated members.

Our Vision

Our journey is fueled by the smiles we create and the lives we touch. We work hand-in-hand with communities to understand their unique needs and co-create sustainable solutions that empower them for generations to come.
